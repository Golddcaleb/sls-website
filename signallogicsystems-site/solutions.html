Job Flow Monitor

Built for: Manufacturing floors · Job shops · Service operations · Ops managers

Real-time visibility into every active job across your operation — from intake to completion. The Job Flow Monitor identifies your production constraint, quantifies the revenue being held up by that constraint, and surfaces the jobs that need immediate attention.

Key Capabilities
  • Constraint identification — which stage is your bottleneck
  • Revenue at risk calculation — dollars delayed by your constraint
  • Upstream cascade analysis — total revenue blocked, not just stalled jobs
  • Job-level delay tracking with priority ranking
  • Executive-ready diagnostic report output

Works with: Any ERP that exports job or work order data as CSV

PO Tracking & Automated Follow-Up

Built for: Procurement teams · Operations managers · Supply chain leads

Automatically monitors your open purchase orders and triggers follow-up communications when lead time constraints shift. Instead of manually chasing suppliers and customers, SLS watches your PO data and sends templated emails at the right moment — so nothing slips through the cracks.

Key Capabilities
  • Longest lead time constraint monitoring
  • Automatic or prompted follow-up emails using your templates
  • Supplier and customer notification logic
  • Delay detection based on promised vs. actual dates
  • Daily digest of PO status changes

Works with: Any ERP or CRM that exports PO data as CSV
