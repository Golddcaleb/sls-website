The Process

How It Works

A simple, secure process that plugs into your existing tools and delivers operational clarity — fast.

Step by Step

From your ERP to operational insight

1

Export

Export a CSV from your ERP or job management system. We work with any system that can produce a standard data export — no custom integrations, no IT involvement, no new software.

Supported: JobBOSS, Epicor, Fishbowl, QuickBooks, custom spreadsheets, and any CSV-exporting system.

2

Submit

Send your export to SLS via secure upload or email. For ongoing monthly service, this becomes an automated scheduled export that runs without manual effort on your end.

3

Process

Your raw data enters our processing engine. It is cleaned, normalized, and analyzed against your configured workflow logic. No raw data is stored — your data is processed and the output is returned. The calculation engine stays on our side.

Your raw operational data is never stored by Signal Logic Systems. We process it, extract the signals, and return the results. We hold the calculator — you receive the answer.

4

Signal

You receive a structured diagnostic output: constraint identification, revenue at risk, job-level delay ranking, and automated alerts. For PO tracking, follow-up emails are triggered automatically or queued for your approval before sending.

Data Privacy

Your data stays yours.

No Storage

Raw client data is never stored permanently. Data flows through our processing engine and is not retained after your output is delivered.

No PII

We do not store customer names, job numbers, descriptions, or any personally identifiable information unless explicitly agreed in writing.

Snapshot-Based

Derived metrics are retained for dashboard continuity (60-day default). On termination, all retained data is deleted within 30 days.

Who It's For

Two kinds of clients. One platform.

Direct Manufacturing Clients

You run a manufacturing operation and want faster, clearer visibility into what's slowing you down — without hiring more staff or replacing your ERP. SLS plugs into what you already use and surfaces the answers your team needs to move faster.

Consulting Firms

You advise manufacturing clients and want a faster, more objective way to diagnose operational problems and quantify improvement opportunities. SLS gives your engagements a structured diagnostic baseline — backed by data, not just observation.
